Mental Health Awareness

Your mental health is as important as your physical health. Here are some tips for better mental well-being:

  • 🧘 Practice meditation or deep breathing
  • 💬 Talk to friends or family when stressed
  • 🏃‍♂️ Exercise regularly
  • 😴 Get 7–8 hours of sleep

General Well-being Tips

  • 🥗 Eat a balanced diet
  • 🚰 Drink enough water
  • ☀️ Spend time outdoors in fresh air
  • 📚 Keep learning and stay positive
